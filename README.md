# PC Data Warehouse Project 

This repository contains a SQL-based PC sales data engineering project. 

## Source Data

- Main raw data file: `csv_files/1772542271737_pc_data (2).csv`
- Row count: ~10,000 rows
  

  Geographic Information: Continent, Country or State, Province or City
  Store Information: Shop Name, Shop Age
  PC-Spec Information: PC Make, PC Model, Storage Type, RAM, Storage Capacity
  Customer details: Customer Name, Customer Surname, Customer Contact Number, Customer Email Address
  Salesperson informations: Sales Person Name, Sales Person Department
  Sales Information: Cost Price, Sale Price, Payment Method, Discount Amount, Purchase Date, Ship   Date, Finance Amount, Credit Score, Channel, Priority, Cost of Repairs, Total Sales per Employee, PC Market Price
